# Diagramatix — Quick Reminder Sheet 9

## Diagram Review

Diagram Review is a structured, sign-off workflow: a diagram owner sends a diagram to chosen reviewers, each reviewer marks it up with comments and then Approves or Declines, and the owner sees the responses come back. It gives process models a proper review trail before they are published. Use it whenever a diagram needs a second pair of eyes or formal endorsement.

### Sending for review (owner)

- Open **Send for Review** and pick a **Collaboration Group** you own or belong to, then choose the accepted members who should review.
- Add a review **objective** and a **due date**. This creates one review per group; reviewers are drawn from the review's own membership, not from project sharing.
- Each reviewer is notified that a diagram is waiting for them.

### Reviewing (reviewer)

- Open the diagram from your **"Received for Review"** tile. It opens in **Review Mode** — shown by a pink banner across the top.
- The palette gains a **Review Comment** — a pink sticky note. Drag it **onto an element** to attach a comment there; it is pre-filled with your name and email and auto-linked to that element. Drop it on blank canvas for a general comment.
- Add as many comments as you need, then use the banner to **Approve**, **Submit comments**, or **Decline to review**.

### Statuses

- **Reviewer status**: pending → in-progress → submitted, or approved / declined-to-review.
- **Review status**: open → resubmitted → closed.

### Back with the owner

- The owner sees returned comments on the canvas and can **filter them by commenter** or **hide all comments** to declutter. Comments are ordinary canvas elements, so the owner can delete them once actioned.
- Address the feedback, then re-send for another round or move to publishing.

---
*Note: This is the human sign-off workflow. It is separate from the automatic rule scanner that steps through layout-rule issues — that one is not part of Send for Review.*
